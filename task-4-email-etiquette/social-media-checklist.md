**Social Media Do's and Don'ts**

✅ **Do’s**:

* **Think before you post, first ask yourself if it reflects who you are.**
* **Keep your profiles respectful and appropriate for academic and future career opportunities.**
* **Verify information before sharing, which avoid spreading misinformation.**
* **Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.**
* **Engage positively, support others and maintain healthy online interactions.**

❌ **Don'ts**:

* **Don’t share personal details like your address, phone number, or passwords.**
* **Avoid posting content that could be offensive, harmful, or can be misunderstood.**
* **Don’t get involved in any online arguments or negativity.**
* **Avoid sharing your daily life or sensitive information.**
* **Don’t ignore the long-term impact, what you post can stay online forever.**
